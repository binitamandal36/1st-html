"The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People" by Stephen R. Covey provides a transformative guide for personal and professional success.
It emphasizes proactive thinking, prioritization, effective communication, collaboration, and personal growth.
The first three habits help older people develop independence, while habits four through six help people establish relationships where they can work together to achieve goals.
